How to Play

Beat the dealer by getting a hand value closer to 21 without going over. Cards 2-10 are face value, J/Q/K are 10, Aces are 11 or 1.

Drag chips from the tray to the betting circles. Main bet is required, side bets are optional. Click DEAL to start.
